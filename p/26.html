Vlog Competition: My Life During Epidemic Warning Level 3

 

Share your creativity and win NTD 700!

The Office of International Affairs invites all the international students to share your creativity by submitting a short clip to record or describe your life after Epidemic Warning Level 3 announcement and you may have a chance to win NTD 700!

Who can join: Full-time international students currently enrolled at Taiwan Tech.

How to join: Email your Name, Student ID No., Email Address, Mobile Phone No. and mp4 file to doristeng@mail.ntust.edu.tw before 12:00, June 14, 2021.

Length of the short clip: Maximum 4 minutes.

Topic of the short clip: The topics of the short clip include but not limit to the following ones:

  1. What is the difference between your normal life and life after warning level-3? How your deal with it?
  2. How do you kill time when you have to stay in the dormitory?
  3. How to look stylish while wearing a face mask? How many kinds of face masks do you have?
  4. What are the PROS of wearing a mask?
  5. Promote the epidemic prevention measures: wear masks indoors and outdoors, avoid gatherings, take your food away, etc.
  6. Create a new topic yourself!

Please note:

  1. Ensure that image and sound quality is sufficient, as Taiwan Tech might use your video for promotional purposes.
  2. Group submissions with maximum 2 people.

Prizes:

  1. First Prize: NTD 700 (NTD 140 withholding tax)
  2. Second Prize: NTD 500 (NTD 100 withholding tax)
  3. Third Prize: NTD 300 (NTD 60 withholding tax)
  4. Fourth to Tenth Prizes: NTD 100 (NTD 20 withholding tax)
  5. Participation gift: All the participation will get a small thank-you gift even not in the top 10.

Important Dates:

  1. May 25, 2021: Announcement
  2. June 14, 2021 12:00: Entry submission deadline
  3. June 15-19, 2021: Primary selection by OIA.
  4. June 21-25, 2021: The top 10 entries select by OIA will be broadcasted on Facebook and YouTube. The entry that receives most “Likes” on YouTube will be the winner.
