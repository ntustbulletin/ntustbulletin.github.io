【公告】2021/06/26圖書館因校內施工停電，電腦系統暫停服務公告 Announcement：The library computer systems are scheduled to shut down on this coming Saturday (6/26)


為因應全校施工停電而圖書館亦將一併停電，本館館藏系統及周邊伺服器將一併關閉。
The reason of this shutdown is due to the scheduled power cut in the whole campus.

 

關閉時間：2021年6月26日(週六) 上午08:00至晚間06:00
Time of shutdown: 2021 June 26, 08:00 a.m. to 06:00 p.m.

 

暫停服務：圖書館網站、館藏查詢、預約與續借、圖書推薦系統、機構典藏系統、博碩士論文系統、討論小間暨視聽區預約系統、校外連線電子書/電子期刊服務、新進館藏跑馬燈服務、學科網頁、暢銷書/經典書系統、論文比對系統(Turnitin 帳號申請)、書香獎系統、電子資源系統、校外人士登入系統、報名系統Temporarily Suspended Services: The library web server, Sierra system (including the services of borrowing books, returning books, bibliography queries, and database searching), recommending system, Institutional Repository of Taiwan Tech, e-Thesis, Room Reservation System, and Off-campus Internet connection services will be suspended till the power resumes.

 

不便之處，尚祈見諒。
We apologize for any inconvenience.
