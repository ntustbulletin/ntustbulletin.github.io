【活動取消通知】國際週活動 (May 17~21) Culture Fair 2021 Is Cancelled

同學們好：

因疫情持續升溫，基於健康安全考量，原訂於5月17日至5月21日在本校大用工坊舉行之文化週活動（含：赴海外交換計畫申請說明會）暫停辦理，並擇日再行舉辦。延期確切日期將視疫情發展而定，請密切注意國際處官網。

造成不便敬請見諒，也呼籲大家一同落實防疫政策，謝謝！

國際事務處

Dear all students,

Due to severe spread of Covid-19 in Taiwan, all the activities in culture fair (held at Da-Yong workshop for next whole week) will be suspended temporarily. The event would be hosted another day. Please pay a close attention to the OIA webpage for further information.

We apologize for all the inconvenience caused.

OIA, Taiwan Tech
