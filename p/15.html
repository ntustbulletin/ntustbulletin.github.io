尊重智慧財產權宣導 / Promotion of Respect for Intellectual Property Rights

各位老師好：

請加強宣導尊重智慧財產權觀念，並提醒、輔導學生使用正版教科書，勿非法影印書籍、教材，以免侵害他人著作權。

發現學生有疑似非法影印行為時，應提醒或制止，無效時應通報學校處理。

宣導簡報檔如附檔，敬請配合宣導。

教務處敬上

Dear teachers：

Please strengthen the promotion of respect for the concept of intellectual property rights, and remind students to use original textbooks. Do not illegally copy books and teaching materials to avoid infringing the copyrights of others.

When students are found to be suspected of illegal photocopying, they should be reminded or be reported to the school for handling if multiple reminders are invalid

For more information, please refer to the presentation file attached below.

Office of Academic Affair
