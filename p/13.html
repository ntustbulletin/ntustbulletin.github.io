【活動轉知】FAITH Competition 2021 of Petra Christian University

 

Dear students,

Petra Christian University would like to share some information on the FAITH Competition 2021 hosted by the Faculty of Industrial Engineering.

To all fellow students, we would like to inform you about the FAITH International Competition "Its impact on Industrial Revolution 4.0 and Future of Work” which was held by the Faculty of Industrial Technology Petra Christian University. Faith International Competition is divided into 4 different types of categories :

  1. Informatics: Design a web-based application (website) that has high appeal and access potential during COVID-19 pandemic, accompanied by making a poster and a video to present the design and the mechanism of the web-based application created.
  2. Industrial Engineering: Conduct research based on case study, which in this case means that the research is done by referring to statistics and other supporting data available online.
  3. Mechanical Engineering: Design a product which could optimize the implementation of COVID-19 health protocol or increase productivity during COVID-19 pandemic.
  4. Electrical Engineering: Design a product which could optimize the implementation of COVID-19 health protocol or respond to some changes in industry 4.0 revolution during COVID-19 pandemic.

(Notes: Posters and videos are sufficient to explain the product clearly)

Each team consists of 2-3 participants, students outside the following major are welcome to join. 

The registration is free, and make sure to fill in the required data during registration. 

For further details visit faith.petra.ac.id and you can download the master guide book of Faith International Competition 2021 provided at the registration website  http://faith.petra.ac.id/ . 

You can win up to a total of $4500 USD approximate IDR 64 MILLION. Don’t miss out on this chance!
