【OIA】"Speak It Out"--Level Up Your Language

 

 

Guarani, Urdu, Bahasa Indonesia, Tamil, Vietnamese … Do you know how many different languages are spoken on Taiwan Tech Campus? Would you like to share your native language? The Office of International Affairs invites you to join the “Speak It Out” activity - “Speak it out”: Teach your mother tongue, learn another language!

How to join?

  1. Please complete the registration form and submit it before 11:00 am June 15th, 2021.

* Registration form: https://forms.gle/UAmGCs3gawELs3PD8

   * Maximum number of participants: 10 people

  1. E-mail your Instagram post to yenyu@mail.ntust.edu.tw before 11:00 am June 22st, 2021.
  2. E-mail your teaching video (mp4 file) to yenyu@mail.ntust.edu.tw before 11:00 am June 29th, 2021.

Who can join?

International students at Taiwan Tech who are willing to share their native language.

What to do and how to receive the NTD 500 reward?

Step 1：Create an Instagram post to advertise your class. Share “Top 10 Words / The word of the Year / A Pet Phrase / Or any other topic” in your native language.

Step 2：Shoot a 5 -10-minute teaching video on your topic.

Step 3：OIA will post your advertisement on Instagram, and upload your teaching video on YouTube.

Step 4：You will be awarded NTD 500 (100 NTD withholding tax) when you complete all steps before the deadline.

Important Dates：

June 11th：Announcement

June 15th：Entry submission deadline

June 22st：E-mail your IG post

June 29th：E-mail your teaching video

Please Note：Ensure that the image and sound quality is sufficient, as Taiwan Tech reserves the right to use your video for promotional purposes.
