【Wanted】seeking committed part-time tutors form Language Cetner

Hi everyone,

We are seeking committed part-time tutors. If you are interested in teaching English, do not hesitate to contact us!!

Qualifications:

1) CEFR B2 (equivalent to New TOEIC 785) or native speaker

2) Enthusiastic about teaching

3) Energetic and communicative

4) Students who are not going to graduate next semester and who are able to work at least 2 semesters are preferred.

5) International students who have basic Mandarin communication skills and a work permit are preferred.

Responsibilities:

1) Provide walk-in consultation

2) Prepare syllabuses and teaching materials for remedial English classes

3) Teach remedial English classes and build classroom management

4) Assist in organizing and hosting English-learning activities

Required documents:

1) A curriculum vitae (1 page at most, including education, related work experience, your current year, department and expected graduate year)

2) A personal statement (2 pages at most)

3) A photocopy of the score report or certificate of the English proficiency test 

4) A 10-minute English lesson plan (including title, objective, content, time, number of participants and materials)

Application procedure:

1)      Please compile all the application documents into a PDF file, and send it to ntustlc207@gmail.com by Thursday, May 20, 17:00.

2)      Please specify in the email subject “Application for Tutor Position in Language Center_ Your Name”.

3)      An interview will be arranged for the qualified applicants.

 

 

If you have any questions, feel free to come to the Language Center (IB-207) for further information.

Thank you!
