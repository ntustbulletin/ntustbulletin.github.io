7/28及7/29大考中心於本校辦理大學入學指定科目考試 / College Entrance Examination Center holding Advanced Subjects Tests in campus on 7/28 and 7/29

 

敬致臺科大所有教職員工生：

本校受大考中心委託將於7月28日及29日辦理110學年度指定科目考試，本校為臺北五考區。因應嚴重特殊傳染性肺炎疫情，針對本次考試各項防疫規定，請同仁及學生全力配合：

一、7月28日(週三)及7月29日(週四)，除上班及公務所需，建議非必要避免進入校園，減少人群聚集，以維護自身健康。

二、如果必須於7月28日(週三)及7月29日(週四)進入校園，須配合如下：

(一)僅開放基隆路校門口進出。

(二) 7月28日、7月29日早上07:00開始，所有人員須佩戴口罩、憑證進入、進行手部消毒及量測體溫，並請優先讓考生入場量體溫。

臺科大教務處

To all the faculty, staff and students of National Taiwan University of Science and Technology:

On July 28th and 29th, we will be administering the 110th academic year Advanced Subjects Test under the request of College Entrance Examination Center, and we are the fifth examination area in Taipei. In view of the Coronavirus disease, we implore for the cooperation of our colleagues and students in the following ways.

1. For your health, on Wednesday, July 28th and Thursday, July 29th, it is recommended to avoid entering the campus unless for work or business to reduce the gathering of people.

2. If you must enter the campus on Wednesday, July 28 and Thursday, July 29, you must cooperate as follows.

(a) Only the entrance on Keelung Road will be open for entry and exit.

(b) Starting at 07:00 a.m. on July 28 and July 29, all staff must wear masks, enter with their ID cards, undergo hand disinfection and take body temperature. Also, the priority to take body temperature will be granted to the examinees.

Office of Academic Affairs,

National Taiwan University of Science and Technology
