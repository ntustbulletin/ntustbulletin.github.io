【轉知】姊妹校德國慕尼黑工業大學於7/5~7/9舉辦2021 Global Week 線上講座活動，歡迎踴躍參加！ [Forwarded Message]: Virtual TUM Global Week 2021 from July 5 to 9, 2021

 

Would you like to learn more about what TUM is all about when it comes to international affairs and the opportunities available to you?

Join the virtual TUM Global Week hosted by the TUM Global & Alumni Office (TUM G&A) from July 5 to 9, 2021 and learn more about TUM and its offers for international students, doctoral candidates and scientists, about funding opportunities for research projects, as well as flagship partnerships, international alliances of TUM and mentoring programs.

Where will your journey take you? Welcome to the Technical University of Munich (TUM).

Join us on July 08, 2021 for a virtual info session about your exchange opportunities and learn all you need to know about a stay abroad at TUM.

On top of the presentation, a virtual city tour of Munich also awaits you.

On July 09, 2021, we offer an intercultural workshop that prepares you for an exchange in Germany.

The events are is part of the TUM Global Week 2021.

Sign up here: TUM Global Week: https://www.international.tum.de/en/tumglobalweek/
