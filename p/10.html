Journals Suppressed from 2020 JCR Data (2021 release)

 

Dear Readers,

Journals Suppressed from 2020 JCR Data (2021 release) are available.

  1. JCR Suppression Lists 2020

The Journal citation (JCR) helps to find relevant, reputable journals for potential publication of your research based on an analysis of tens of millions of citation connections in the SCIE, SSCI, AHCI, and the ESCI. Journal Impact Factor and 5-Year Impact Factors are not published for the titles listed below due to anomalous citations patterns. These patterns result in a significant distortion of the Journal Impact Factor and rank that does not accurately reflect the journal’s citation performance in the literature. You can check the JCR Suppression Lists before submission.

For more information, you can review Journals Suppressed from 2020 JCR Data (2021 release).

  1. Predatory Journals and Publishers

Predatory publishing involves charging publication fees to authors without checking articles for quality and legitimacy, and without providing editorial and publishing services that legitimate academic journals provide. You can find predatory Journals on Beal's list before submission.

beallslist.net: https://beallslist.net/

  1. The latest ranking of the journals

The Web of Science Group publishes the JCR data of the previous year every summer. You can check the latest ranking of the journal before submitting the paper. The ranking information can be found in the JCR database.
